BTS's V, whose real name is Kim Taehyung, is a multifaceted artist known for his unique voice and charismatic stage presence. Born on December 30, 1995, in Daegu, South Korea, V initially pursued a career in music alongside his studies in arts. He debuted with BTS in 2013, quickly becoming a beloved member of the group. V is often recognized for his deep, soulful vocals and has contributed to the group's diverse sound through various genres, including pop, R&B, and hip-hop. His charm and emotional performances have garnered him a dedicated fanbase, known as ARMY.

In addition to his work with BTS, V has explored solo projects, showcasing his versatility as an artist. He released his first solo song, "Stigma," in 2016, and has since shared tracks like "Sweet Night" for the soundtrack of the series "Itaewon Class." Beyond music, V is known for his artistic sensibilities, having showcased his photography and painting skills. His fashion sense and influence on pop culture further solidify his status as a global icon. With his genuine personality and creative talents, V continues to captivate audiences worldwide, making significant contributions to both BTS and the music industry at large.
